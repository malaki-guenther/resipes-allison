zucchini pie

ingridants

  • 4 cups thinly sliced succhini
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup margarine
  • garlic
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley/2 tbsp parsley flakes
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp basil
  • 1/4 tsp oregano leaves
  • 2 eggs
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese/chedder
  • 2 tsp mustard

steps

  1. cook and stir 4 cups thinly sliced succhini and 1 cup chopped onion 1/4 - 1/2 cup margarine for 10 minuets and garlic
  2. stir in 1/2 cup chopped parsley or 2 tbsp parsley flakes,
    1/2 tsp salt
    1/2 tsp pepper
    garlic powder
    1/4 tsp basil
    1/4 tsp oregano leaves
  3. combine 2 beaten eggs + 8 oz(2 cups)shredded mozzarella cheese (or cheeder)
    • cheese first until melted then egg right before it goes into the crust
  4. stir in zucchini mixture in partially pre cooked whole wheat crust
    • -add tomato at the last second
  5. no --> spread crust with 2 tsp mustard
  6. pour veggie mixture in to crust
  7. bake in 375* oven for 18-20 minuets or until center is set

ham and scalloped potatoes

ingridants

  • 3tbsp butter/margarine
  • 1 small onion
  • 3tbsp flour
  • 1tsp slat
  • 1/4tsp pepper
  • 2,1/2cups milk
  • 6med potatoes-sliced-6cups
  • 1,1/2cups ham-cubed
  • 1tbsp butter|margin
  • cheese

steps

  1. heat ooven to 350*f spray pan w/ cooking spray
  2. melt 3tbsp butter. cook onion in butter for 2 minutes stirring canstantly till tender. strin in flour,salt,and pepper. cook stirring contantly till smooth and bubbly. remove from heat. stir in milk. heat to boiling, stiring constantly. boil and stir for 1min.add cheese
  3. mix potatoes and ham together in dish. pour sauce over potato mixture. cut 1tbsp butter into small pieces and repeat untill you have used 3tbsp of butter or margin; sprinkle over potatoes
  4. cover and bake 30min. uncover and bake 1hr - 1hr + 10min till potatoes are tender. let stand 5min - 10min before serving
    (sauce thickens)

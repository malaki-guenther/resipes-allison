clam chowder

ingredients

  • 4 center-cut bacon strips
  • 2 celery rips - chopped
  • 1 med. onions (1/2 large)chopped
  • 3 small potatoes-peeled+cubed
  • 1 cup water
  • garlic powder
  • 8 oz chicken broth
  • 3 tsp chicken boulion granules
  • pepper
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 cups half and half

steps

  1. cook bacon in pot until done. remove paper towels to drain; set aside.
  2. saute celery and onions in the dripping until tender. add a little butter if need for saute. add garlic and cook 1 min longer
  3. stir in the potatoes, water, chicken broth, bouillon, and pepper.
  4. bring to a boil.reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 15 - 20 minuets or until potatoes are tender.
  5. in a small bowl, combine flo9ur and 1 cup of half and half until smooth. gradually stir into soup. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 1 - 2 minuets or until thickened.
  6. stir in clams and remaining half and half; heat through(do not boil).
  7. cut bacon into small pieces and serve with each bowl.

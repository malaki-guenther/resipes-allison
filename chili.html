chili

ingredients

  • 1 can of putter beans
  • 1 can of light red kidney beans
  • 1 can of dark red kidney beans
  • 1 can of navy beans
  • 1 can of great nothern beans
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 cab if black eyed peas
  • 2 cans diced tomato
  • 1 8 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 box of carroll shelby chili kit
  • any amount of spices from these options
    • onion salt
    • basil
    • garlic salt
    • oregano
    • mrs.dash

steps

  1. brown and drain ground beef. put ground beef, water, diced tomato, big spice packet, and 1/2 of cayenne pepper pack.medium heat. cover
  2. open and rinse all beans.cover and simmer for 25 min.
  3. add can of green chiles with juice(add corn if adding, and other spices). cook for 5 minuets
  4. mix masa. 1/3 cup water total add a little water and a little masa, mix. repeat till masa is incorporated.
  5. add masa to chili
  6. taste and adjust as needed cook tilo you think it's "done".:)

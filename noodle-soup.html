chicken/turckey noodle soup

ingridants

  • 4 cups chopped cooked chicken/turkey meat
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 8 oz noodles
  • 5 cup broth
  • oregano, parsley, spices

steps

  1. melt butter and saute celery and onion in butter.
  2. add chicken/turkey, carrots, broth, and simmer for 30 min
  3. add noodles and simmer for 10 more minuets
    • if you are freezing, do not add noodles freeze with meat/veggies/broth. add noodles when thawed again

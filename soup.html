puppy chow

ingridants

  • 9 cups chex mix
  • 1,1/2 cups semi - sweet chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 - 1/8 cup butter
  • 1,1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups powdered sugar

steps

  1. measures cereal in large bowl. in bowl, microwave chocolate chips, peanut butter and butter for 1 minute. stir. microwave 30 seconds longer or until mixture can be stirred smooth. stir in vanilla
  2. pour mixture over cereal, stirring until evenly coated. let cool to room temperature. put 1/2 of mix in gallon ziploc bag with 1/2 powdered sugar. shake. lay on waxed paper to cool

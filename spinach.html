spinach lasagna

ingredients

  • 2 jars of spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cans mushrooms pieces and stems(8 oz)
  • 2 large containers of cottage cheese(small curd)
  • 3 bell peppers(1 red, 1 green, 1 orange, or red)
  • 1 box lasagna noodles(16 oz)
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 boxes frozen chopped spinach(bird's eye) (10 oz)
  • 6 cups of 6 cheese italian blend

steps

  1. thaw and drain spinach. dice peppers
  2. saute mushrooms and peppers in melted butter. let cool.
  3. cook lasagna noodles. drain and let cool.
  4. in a big bowl mix cottage cheese and spinach. Add about 4 cups of cheese(keep some to put on top for end result)
  5. mix in the cooled peppers and mushrooms.
  6. warm up the spaghetti sauce(add any spices to taste). Lightly layer bottome of pan with sauce
  7. heat oven to 350*
  8. on the table... layout noodles place a row of spinachmixture up the center of the noodle. roll the noodle starting with the end closest to you.
  9. take the rolled noodle and place in the pan. once the pan is full, cover noodles in sauce. Allow room for bubbling.
  10. cover with aluminum foil-oven for 1 hr.(approx.) Take out and sprinkle with cheese.

banna cream pie

crust

ingridants

  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1,1/3 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3-4 tbsp cold water

steps

  1. pre-cook = 375*, prick bottom, bake until light brown, 8 - 10 minutes, cool

filling

ingridants

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch or 1/2 cup flour
  • 3 cups milk(whole)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1,1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp butter/margarine

steps

  1. in med saucepan combine sugar and flour. gradually stir in milk.
  2. cook over medium heat till mixture is thick and bubbly
  3. reduce heat, cook and stir for 2 more minutes. femove from heat.sparate egg yolks from whites set whites aside for meringue(look at meringue cont to see how to make this).
  4. Beat egg yolks lightly with a fork.
  5. Gradually stir in about 1 cupof the hot filling into yolks.
  6. return all to saucepan; bring to a gentle boil.
  7. Cook and stir for 2 minutes more
  8. remove from heat
  9. stir in margarine / butter vanilla -slice bananas into bottom of shell
  10. pour the hot filling into baked shell
  11. evenly spread meringue over filling, seal to edge.
  12. bake at 350* for 15 minutes

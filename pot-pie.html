bisquick pot pie

ingridants

  • 2cups veggies
  • 2cups chicken
  • 1 can cream of chiken
  • 1/2cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1cup of bisquick mix

steps

  1. mix veggies,chicken and cream of chicken soup and put in pie plate
  2. mix bisquick, milk, egg. put over veggie/chicken mix
  3. bake at 400*f for 30min

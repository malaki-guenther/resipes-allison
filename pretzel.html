pretzel bites

ingridants

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1,1/2 tbsp (2 packet) active dry yeast
  • 6 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp butter melted
  • 5 - 5,1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup baking soda
  • 3 cups warm water
  • coarse salt
  • 8 tbsp butter melted

steps

  1. warm up milk in the microwave for about 1,1/2 min it should be about 110*. if it's too hot it will kill the yeast.you should be able to comfortably keep your finger in it
  2. stir in the yeast and one tbsp of brown sugar and let sit for about 10 minutes
  3. put mixture in kitchen aid bowl add butter and remaining sugar. mix.
  4. add flour 1 cup at a time and then add the fine salt
  5. knead for 10 minutes
  6. put in large greased bowl and cover with greased cling wrap
  7. let rise for 1 hour in a warm moist place until doubled in size
  8. preheat oven to 450*
  9. punch down dough and divide. roll reach piece to form a thin rope.
  10. cut the dough into small pieces dip pieces into warm water mixed with baking soda
  11. put pieces on greased baking sheet and sprinkle with coarse salt. bake for 6 - 10 minutes or until browned
  12. dip/dunk each pretzel in melted butter while hot

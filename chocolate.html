chocolate pretzels

ingridants

  • mini pretzels
  • 8 oz milk chocolate
  • 2 tbsp vegetable shortening
  • decorations

    • white chocolate drizzle:

  • 4 oz white chocolate
  • 1 tbsp shortening

steps

  1. in a medium size bowl add chocolate and shortening.
  2. microwave, stirring every 15 seconds until completely melted.
  3. use a fork to dip pretzels into and out of chocolate. Let excess chocolate drip off place on wax paper to dry
  4. when hardened - re - dip if needed

    5. white chocolate drizzle:

  5. melt 4 oz white chocolate, 1 tbsp shortening - filing with fork over pretzels

buffalo chicken dip

ingridants

  • 2 (10 oz) cans chicken
  • 2 (8 oz) packets cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • dippers of choice
    • bread
    • chips
    • celery
    • etc...

steps

  1. drain canned chicken - put in a sauce pan.
  2. add hot sauce - cook on medium until heated throughout
  3. add cream cheese - stir till blended thoroughly
  4. add ranch dressing - mix
  5. add 1,1/2 cups of cheese and heat until mixed and melted(a few minutes)
  6. pour dip into crock pot - sprinkle rest of cheese on top - keep on low
  7. serve with dippers

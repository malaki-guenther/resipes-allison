insta-pot mac-n-cheese

ingridants

the mac-n-cheese

  • 2,1/2 cups of noodles
  • 2 cups of chicken broth(or bouillon mix)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp salted butter
  • 8 oz shredded chedder cheese
  • 2 oz cheese

instant pot cooking noodles

  • 4,1/2 cups noodles
  • 4 cups broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 9 oz cheese
  • 4 oz cream cheese

steps

  1. add noodles, broth, water, and butter to insta-pot. stir together cook on high pressure for 6 minuets
  2. quick release presure and wait until pin goes down, remove lid and stir, turn on saute
  3. add cream cheese and chedder cheese mix till melted suase will thicken as it stands

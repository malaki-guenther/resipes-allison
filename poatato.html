potato enchiladas

ingridants

  • potatoes
  • chicken/ground beef
  • cor
  • green beans
  • toria's
  • enchilada sauce
  • cheese(cheddar)
  • milk
  • butter
  • garlic salt

steps

mashed potatoes

  1. boil water, and cut the potatoes
  2. boil potatoes till they are soft
  3. mash potatoes and mix in garlic salt, milk, and butter

filing

  1. cook meat
  2. mix in with corn and green beans
  3. mix in potato a's and a handful of cheese

enchiladas

  1. preheat oven to 350*
  2. put filling in the middle of tortie and fold tortie in a roll, place in pan
  3. put enchilada on bottom of pan and sides
  4. repeat steps 2 and 3 until the pan is full
  5. layer the enchilada sauce on top
  6. sprinkle cheese on top of the sauce
  7. put in oven and cook for 25-30 minutes

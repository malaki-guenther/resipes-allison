tuna casserole

ingridants

  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cans tuna
  • noodles of choice(cooked and drain)
  • 2 differnt vegetables (canned or frozen)

steps

  1. heat oven to 400*
  2. mix milk, tuna, soup, vegetables in a bowl
  3. add noodles and stir together
  4. bake for 20 minuets or until hot and bubbling
